Meet Shane Hagan

A Lifetime Dedicated to Roofing Excellence

For over three decades, Shane Hagan has been the trusted name in roofing across Fountain Hills, Rio Verde, and Scottsdale. What started as an apprenticeship turned into a lifelong passion for protecting Arizona homes.

Shane built Fountain Hills Roofing on a simple philosophy: do honest work at fair prices, and treat every roof like it's your own. That commitment to integrity has earned us the trust of thousands of homeowners over 40+ years.

Unlike many roofing companies, we own all our equipment — dump trucks, forklifts, and specialized tools. This means we never subcontract work, giving you better quality control, faster timelines, and lower costs.
