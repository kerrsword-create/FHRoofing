Maintenance Guide

When Should You Re-Roof?

Understanding your roof's lifespan helps you plan ahead and avoid costly emergency repairs.

Tile Roofs

20-30

Years between re-roofing. Tile is durable but the underlayment beneath degrades over time and needs replacement.

Flat Roofs

5-7

Years between re-coating. Regular coating maintenance prevents leaks and extends the life of your flat roof system.
